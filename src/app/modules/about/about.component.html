My stack for this project:

There are lots of reasons to choose one stack over another depending on the project. The goal was to build a game that I would play with pleasure, being satisfied with its appearance and operation. The side effect was a challenge sent to friends who were eagerly competing with each other for the best result.

I was looking ahead at the future of my project so I have chosen Angular to learn how to build an app with the possibility of vertical scalability. Game mechanism and model of blocks' behaviour was built from zero in Pixi.js creation engine.
